### Identify needs, pain points, and opportunities in order to create human experiences that matter.

## Better understand people

The principles of IBM Design Research guide diverse teams to continually increase their understanding of the people they serve and the world around them. By pairing people’s needs with those of the business, we can differentiate in the market.
